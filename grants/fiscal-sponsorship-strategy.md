## Recommended Path: Fractured Atlas First, Open Circle as Partner

**Fractured Atlas** is the fastest route to grant eligibility:
- Rolling applications, 10-day turnaround
- $10/month + 8% admin fee
- Arts-focused, established with funders
- Say Why qualifies (artistic project with public benefit)
- Once approved: can accept tax-deductible donations + apply for grants under their 501(c)(3)

**Open Circle Foundation** is a peer relationship, not a fiscal sponsor:
- Small org ($50K fundraise for 2026)
- Direct-service, not set up for fiscal sponsorship
- Shared mission — better as a collaboration partner
- Could become a co-applicant on future grants
