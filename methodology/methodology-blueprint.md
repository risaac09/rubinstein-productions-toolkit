# Rubinstein Productions — Methodology Blueprint
*Working Document | Created: 2026-03-30*
*Purpose: Consolidate the RP methodology into a client-legible, market-ready framework*

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Rubinstein Productions is a facilitation and film practice. The methodology creates conditions where mission-driven professionals can recognize and express what's true about their work — the "inside version" that institutional systems routinely filter into something accurate and inert.

**The transformation:** The client moves from systemic alienation from honest expression → to embodied recognition of their own truth → to a professional artifact (video, narrative) that can travel without them in the room.

**The mechanism:** Witnessing. Isaac facilitates. The camera witnesses. The client learns to witness themselves. The inside version surfaces not because the client was brave, but because the conditions made recognition possible.

**The offering is anti-extractive by design.** The camera ships to the client. They film themselves. Everything is returned. Nothing is extracted.

---

## THE METHODOLOGY: FOUR PHASES

### Phase 1: The Container
*What enters the space must be safe enough for truth to surface.*

**Goal:** Establish relational safety and institutional permission simultaneously.

**What Isaac does:**
- Facilitated intake using PIPA (Participatory Impact Pathways Analysis) principles
- Co-creates a logic model with the client: inputs, activities, outputs, outcomes
- Maps the project in the language the client's organization already speaks
- Creates the institutional cover that allows deeper work to proceed

**What the client experiences:**
- The process feels structured and legible to their professional context
- They can explain to leadership what's happening and why
- Relational trust begins building through the quality of listening

**Deliverable:** Logic model and project roadmap

**Duration:** 1-2 sessions (Tier 1 stays here; Tiers 2-3 proceed)

**Mirrored DL equivalent:** Setting up the vault — creating the architecture that will hold the metabolic process.

---

### Phase 2: The Excavation
*What's alive beneath the institutional script?*

**Goal:** Surface the "inside version" — what the client actually knows, believes, and would say in trusted company.

**What Isaac does:**
- Facilitated conversations using CBSD (Community Based System Dynamics) principles
- Maps the feedback loops that keep honest expression contained
- Moves from surface (elevator pitch, about page) to depth (what would you say if you knew it would land?)
- Identifies where the living thing is being filtered into something inert

**What the client experiences:**
- Recognition: "That's it — that's the thing I've been circling"
- The inside version becomes conscious and articulable
- The structural nature of their communication gap becomes visible (it's the system, not them)

**Deliverable:** Clear narrative direction + map of what's been containing the honest version

**Duration:** 2-3 sessions

**Mirrored DL equivalent:** The encounter — meeting information as event, not object. The somatic signal that marks what's alive.

---

### Phase 3: The Performance
*The camera enters. The client holds it.*

**Goal:** Capture the inside version on camera — on the client's terms, in their space, with their hands.

**What Isaac does:**
- Ships camera and lens to the client
- Facilitates remotely while the client films themselves
- Minimal constraints: natural light, eye level, say what's true
- Holds the witness position — listening, co-regulating, not directing

**What the client experiences:**
- Embodied encounter with their own truth. The camera is a witness, not a surveillance device.
- The nervous system habituates to being observed. Performance drops away. What remains is the real thing.
- They learn the technology. They hold the means of production. Agency is structural, not aspirational.
- The gap between the inside version and the performed version becomes visible in their own footage

**Deliverable:** Raw footage — shot by the client, on their own terms

**Duration:** 1-3 filming sessions over 1-2 weeks

**Mirrored DL equivalent:** The metabolic process itself — sitting with what's unresolved, not organizing it, letting the living thing surface.

---

### Phase 4: The Translation
*The living thing becomes an artifact that can travel.*

**Goal:** Turn raw truth into a professional video that carries the inside version to audiences who weren't in the room.

**What Isaac does:**
- Produces a 3-minute brand video from the client's raw footage
- Returns ALL footage, performance data, and final video to the client
- Delivers Bilingual Dashboard (Tier 2+): Royal metrics (institutional ROI language) + Nomadic indicators (relational health, emergence)
- Nothing is kept. The client owns everything.

**What the client experiences:**
- The story can now travel without them. Funders, audiences, websites — the artifact does the work.
- They see themselves honestly on camera, often for the first time
- The capacity for honest expression has been practiced, not just talked about
- The witness has been partially internalized — they know what it feels like to hold truth in front of a camera

**Deliverable:** 3-minute brand video + all raw footage returned + all data returned + Bilingual Dashboard (Tier 2+)

**Duration:** 1-2 weeks post-filming

**Mirrored DL equivalent:** The output — the note that stays alive, the synthesis that surprises you, the framework that generates.

## SERVICE TIERS

### Tier 1: The Mirror ($500–$1,500)
**Phases included:** Container only (Phase 1)
**What it is:** A single 90-minute facilitated session + narrative synthesis
**Who it's for:** Individual professionals — consultants, researchers, nonprofit leaders
**What they get:** The experience of being listened to at a depth that surfaces the inside version. A written "Narrative Synthesis" — their honest version, on paper.
**Goal:** Low friction. High trust. Client experiences the process before committing to production.
**Revenue characteristics:** High volume potential, low delivery cost, pipeline builder for Tier 2

### Tier 2: The Map ($5,000–$12,000)
**Phases included:** All four phases (Container → Excavation → Performance → Translation)
**What it is:** The full methodology, 4-6 weeks
**Who it's for:** Small nonprofits, SMEs, teams in transition
**What they get:** Facilitated sessions + camera shipped + client films + Isaac produces a 3-minute brand video + all footage returned + Bilingual Dashboard (Lite)
**Goal:** Solve a specific communication problem. The story can travel without the founder in the room.
**Revenue characteristics:** Core revenue driver. Most engagements should land here.

### Tier 3: The Territory ($4,000–$8,000/month)
**Phases included:** Repeated cycles of all four phases over 3-6 months
**What it is:** Creative partnership retainer
**Who it's for:** Established organizations, foundations, impact networks
**What they get:** Full facilitation arc + 3-6 produced videos + Bilingual Dashboard (Full) + quarterly review
**Goal:** Deep integration. The organization's narrative becomes self-sustaining. The witness function is internalized by the team.
**Revenue characteristics:** Highest LTV. Requires demonstrated proof of concept (ideally a Tier 2 engagement first).
