## Risk flags

- **Scheduling drift:** if any excavation session slips, the whole synthesis chain compresses. Mitigation: confirm all 3 session dates by Apr 15 (end of Day 2).
- **Board date unknown:** confirmed by Week 3 check-in at latest. If board meets earlier than May 9, compress Phase 3 prep.
- **Stakeholder churn:** if Oakside swaps an excavation participant mid-sprint, voice guide synthesis risks fragmentation. Name this in kickoff.
- **Scope creep:** any request beyond the 4 deliverables → parked to Phase 2 scope conversation, not absorbed.
