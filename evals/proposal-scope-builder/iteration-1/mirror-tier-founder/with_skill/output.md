## Understanding Your Situation

Here's what I'm hearing between the lines:

You're fluent in your own field. When the room is technical, your voice is native — it moves at the speed of your actual thinking. Then a funder or partner shows up, and something translates in real-time from *your* language into *their* expected language. The translation is so fast you barely notice it happening, but you feel the result: polished, hollow, slightly off-body.

This is a classic **systemic alienation from honest expression** pattern for solo founders, especially scientists and practitioners who moved from inside an institution to running their own shop. The nervous system learned early that funders speak a certain dialect, and it now reaches for that dialect automatically — even when your authentic voice would land harder.

The work here isn't to build a new voice. It's to stop the unconscious translation. To identify what gets shaved off in the funder-facing version, and to build a founder voice that doesn't require you to shape-shift at the door.

This is exactly what the Mirror tier is designed for.

## Proposed Engagement: Mirror — 3-Session Voice Excavation Arc

**Format:** Three 1:1 sessions, 90 minutes each, over 4–6 weeks.
**Modality:** Video call (recorded, on-camera — the camera acts as co-regulatory witness, not performance pressure).

### Session 1 — Excavation (Week 1)
We locate the inside version of your voice. I ask the questions that get you talking the way you talk when no funder is in the room. We identify the specific phrases, cadences, and framings that are *yours* — and I mirror back the places where your language involuntarily shifts register.

**You leave with:** An audio/text record of your native founder voice captured in the session, plus a first map of where the translation-reflex fires.

### Session 2 — Performance (Weeks 2–3)
We work live on the exact scenarios where your voice goes hollow — funder pitches, partner intros, conference stages. You perform them. I reflect where the body stays with you and where it leaves. We rebuild those moments from the inside out, keeping your voice load-bearing all the way through.

**You leave with:** Reworked language for 2–3 high-stakes contexts you're actually walking into.

### Session 3 — Translation (Weeks 4–6)
We consolidate. I translate what we've found into a founder-voice reference document — your own language, captured and organized so you can return to it when the translation-reflex tries to fire again. This is not a brand guide. It's a mirror you can hold up to yourself.

**You leave with:** A Bilingual Dashboard — your internal scientist voice on one side, your founder voice on the other, with the bridge language that keeps them coherent.

### What This Is Not
- Not a strategy sprint (no competitive positioning, no messaging framework imposed from outside)
- Not content production (no deliverables for public consumption)
- Not coaching in the traditional sense (I'm not teaching you to communicate "better" — I'm helping you stop translating)

### Zero-Measurement Zone
Because sessions are recorded on camera, a note: the recordings belong to you. They exist so you can return to the moments where your voice landed. They will never be used for marketing, case study, or any purpose without your explicit written consent. The camera's job in this work is witness, not surveillance.
