## Pricing Recommendation

This falls squarely into **Tier 2 — Map (Narrative Strategy Sprint)**, half-day format.

**Published range for half-day Map engagement: $3,000 - $5,000**

For a leadership team workshop, I'd anchor at **$5,000** and here's why:

- **Group facilitation for a leadership team is high-stakes work.** You're not working with one person — you're holding space for organizational dynamics, power structures, and collective narrative.
- **Prep time is real labor.** A half-day workshop requires at minimum a full day of prep (understanding the org, designing the session arc, customizing exercises). Scoping labor is labor.
- **The floor rate is $500/hr.** A half-day is roughly 4 hours of delivery, but when you account for prep, travel, and the 5-day post-latency before deliverables, you're looking at 8-10 hours of total commitment. At $500/hr floor, that's $4,000-$5,000 minimum.
- **Anchor high, negotiate down.** If they push back, you can reduce scope (e.g., drop a post-session deliverable or follow-up session) rather than reduce your rate. Never discount the rate itself.

**Quote: $5,000 for the half-day workshop.**

If they want a post-workshop deliverable (Narrative Audit summary, recording, follow-up session), that's a separate line item or bumps you toward $6,000-$7,000.

## Recommended Next Steps

1. **Quote $5,000** for the half-day workshop, delivered as a Narrative Strategy Sprint (Map tier). Frame the deliverable clearly — what they get is the facilitated session itself plus whatever bounded follow-up you choose to include.
2. **Ask clarifying questions before finalizing:** How large is the leadership team? What's the organizational context (transition, fundraising plateau, strategic pivot)? This shapes session design and confirms avatar fit.

Do not discount below $3,000 under any framing. If their budget is significantly below floor, offer a referral rather than undercutting your rate.
