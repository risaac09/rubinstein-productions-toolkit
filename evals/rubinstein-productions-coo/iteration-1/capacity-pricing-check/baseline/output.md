## Pricing Recommendation

For a half-day (4-hour) voice workshop facilitated for a leadership team, I recommend quoting **$3,500 - $5,000**, depending on the following factors:

**Base rate logic:**
- A half-day facilitation engagement typically runs 4 hours of delivery time plus 4-6 hours of prep (custom design, pre-interviews if included, materials).
- At a blended rate of $250-350/hr across prep + delivery, that lands in the $3,500-$5,000 range.
- Conference-sourced leads tend to have higher willingness to pay since they already saw your work and self-selected.

**Push toward $5,000 if:**
- The team is 8+ people
- They want pre-workshop intake calls with individual leaders
- Custom exercises or follow-up materials are expected
- The company is well-funded (founder context suggests this)
- Travel is required (add travel costs on top)

**$3,500 floor if:**
- Smaller team (4-6 people)
- Straightforward workshop format you have run before
- Local, no travel
- You want to build the relationship for repeat work

**Do not go below $3,000.** A half-day of your time plus prep is a full week-equivalent of attention. Underpricing signals lack of confidence in the exact methodology they saw at the conference and liked enough to reach out about.

## Capacity Check

With 3 active projects, the key question is whether next month has a week where you can do 4-6 hours of prep and then hold a half-day clear for delivery.

**You can likely fit it if:**
- Your 3 active projects are not all in simultaneous high-intensity phases (e.g., shoots, delivery deadlines)
- You block prep across 2-3 shorter sessions rather than needing one contiguous block
- The workshop date falls on a week without other major deliverables

**Flag it as tight if:**
- Two or more of the 3 projects have deliverables due the same week
- You are already feeling stretched on recovery or creative energy
- Any of the active projects are in an unpredictable phase (client feedback loops, edit rounds)

**Recommendation:** Say yes, but propose 2-3 specific date options that fall in your lighter weeks next month. Do not let the founder pick any date -- you pick the windows that work. This protects your capacity without saying no to good-fit revenue.
