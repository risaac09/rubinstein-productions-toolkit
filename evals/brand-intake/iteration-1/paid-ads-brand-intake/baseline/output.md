# Brand Intake: Lumenhouse Candles

Welcome aboard. Before we build any paid-ads strategy, I need to understand Lumenhouse as a brand, a business, and a voice. Below is a structured intake — please answer as completely as you can. Skip any question you genuinely don't have an answer to yet (that's useful signal too), but aim to provide context wherever possible.

---

## 1. Business Snapshot

1. **One-sentence description.** How would you describe Lumenhouse Candles to a stranger at a dinner party?
2. **Founding story.** Who started it, when, and why? What itch were you scratching that existing candle brands weren't?
3. **Current stage.** Revenue range (monthly or annual), team size, years in market, full-time vs. side-project.
4. **DTC channel mix.** What % of revenue comes from your Shopify (or equivalent) site vs. Amazon, wholesale, retail, markets, pop-ups?
5. **Top SKUs.** Which 3-5 products drive the majority of revenue? Price points for each.

---

## 2. Product & Craft

6. **Wax, wick, fragrance.** What do you use (soy, coconut, beeswax, paraffin, blends)? Cotton/wood wicks? Clean/non-toxic claims? Phthalate-free?
7. **Scent philosophy.** How do you develop scents — in-house perfumer, fragrance house collaboration, sourced oils? What's your POV on scent?
8. **Vessel & packaging.** Glass, ceramic, tin, refillable? Who designs it? Sustainability story?
9. **Price architecture.** Entry price → hero price → premium price. Which tier are you leaning into for paid?
10. **Margin.** Roughly, what's your blended gross margin? (This determines how aggressive we can be with CAC.)

---

## 3. Customer

11. **Who buys.** Describe your best customer in concrete detail — age, gender, life stage, where they live, what else they buy, what they care about. Are you seeing repeat buyers or mostly one-time?
12. **Why they buy.** Is it gifting? Self-care? Home aesthetic? Ritual? Scent memory? All of the above — but which dominates?
13. **Reviews.** Can you send me 20-30 of your best reviews (5-star, with narrative)? And any 1-2 star reviews with real feedback. These are gold for ad copy.
14. **Objections.** What makes people hesitate before buying? (Price, shipping, "I have too many candles already," scent uncertainty online, etc.)

---

## 4. Brand Voice & Positioning

15. **Three words** that describe Lumenhouse's voice.
16. **Three words** that do NOT describe it. (What do you refuse to sound like?)
17. **Brand you admire** (candle or otherwise) for voice/aesthetic, and one you'd never want to be compared to.
18. **Positioning.** Finish this sentence: "Lumenhouse is the candle brand for people who ________, unlike ________ who ________."
19. **Visual identity.** Send your logo files, brand guide (if exists), product photography, lifestyle imagery, and any video assets. Instagram handle + TikTok handle.

---

## 5. Competitive Landscape

20. **Direct competitors.** Name 3-5 DTC candle brands you benchmark against (or that show up in the same customer's cart).
21. **Adjacent competitors.** What non-candle brands is your customer buying from? (Fragrance, home, wellness, gifting.)
22. **Differentiation.** What do you do that none of them do? Be specific — not "quality" or "craftsmanship."

---

## 6. Paid Ads History & Current State

23. **Current ad platforms.** Meta (Facebook/Instagram), TikTok, Google (Search/Shopping/PMax), Pinterest, others?
24. **Monthly ad spend.** Current, and target for the next 90 days.
25. **Current performance.** Blended ROAS, Meta ROAS, CAC, AOV, LTV (if you know it).
26. **Best-performing ads to date.** Send screenshots/links to top 3 creatives and what made them work.
27. **Worst performers.** What flopped? Why do you think so?
28. **Pixel & tracking.** Meta Pixel + CAPI installed? GA4? Triple Whale / Northbeam / other attribution tool?
29. **Account access.** Who currently manages Meta Ads Manager, the Shopify store, the pixel? Is access ready to share?

---

## 7. Offer & Funnel

30. **Hero offer.** What's the offer we're leading with in ads? (First-order discount, bundle, free shipping threshold, gift-with-purchase, subscription?)
31. **Email/SMS.** Klaviyo (or other)? List size? Welcome flow performance? Abandoned cart flow?
32. **Landing pages.** Do you run traffic to PDPs, collection pages, or dedicated LPs? Any you're proud of?
33. **Post-purchase.** Do you have a subscription option, refill program, or repeat-purchase mechanism?

---

## 8. Goals & Constraints

34. **90-day goal.** What does a win look like by July? (Revenue target, new-customer count, ROAS floor.)
35. **12-month goal.** Where do you want Lumenhouse to be a year from now?
36. **Budget flexibility.** If paid proves out, is there headroom to scale? What's the ceiling?
37. **Non-negotiables.** Anything we absolutely cannot say, show, or claim? Compliance issues, past legal exposure, founder preferences?
38. **Seasonality.** Which months crush it for you? Which are soft? Q4 gifting peak?

---

## 9. Creative Assets & Workflow

39. **Who creates content?** In-house, agency, freelancers, UGC creators? What's the current volume (posts/week)?
40. **UGC library.** Do you have a creator roster or UGC rights to customer content?
41. **Review platform.** Yotpo, Okendo, Judge.me, Loox?
42. **Turnaround expectations.** How fast can you approve creative? Who's the final decision-maker?

---

## 10. Handoff

43. **Primary contact** at Lumenhouse — name, role, email, preferred comms (Slack, email, Loom).
44. **Anyone else** I should loop in? (Co-founder, COO, email/retention lead.)

---

**Next step after you respond:** I'll do a web research pass on Lumenhouse (site audit, current ads via Meta Ad Library, top competitors' active creative, review-mining), then send back a brand context document with my initial creative-strategy hypotheses and recommended first test batches for Meta. That becomes the foundation every ad builds from.

Send answers back in any format — doc, email, voice memo, bullet list. Whatever's fastest.
