## Say Why Alignment Module (RP-specific — Q9, Q10, Q11)

These are the three diagnostic questions that determine Say Why fit and pricing tier. Answer as best you can based on conversations you've had with the prospect — if you haven't asked them yet, note which you'd need to surface in a discovery call.

**Q9 (Voice integrity):**
> "When was the last time your public messaging felt like it actually came from *inside* the work — not written FOR an audience, but written FROM the lived experience of doing what you do?"

**Q10 (Inside version):**
> "What do you know to be true about your mission that you've never said out loud publicly — because you haven't found the words, or because saying it would cost something?"

**Q11 (Jargon test):**
> "If you had to describe what you do without using any industry language — without the words your sector uses — how would you say it?"
