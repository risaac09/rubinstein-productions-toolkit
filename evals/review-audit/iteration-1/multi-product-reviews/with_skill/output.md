PRODUCT: Evergreen Diffuser
Reviews analyzed: 4 (Reviews 2, 3, 4, 6) | Discarded (score 1): 0
─────────────────────────────────────

BUCKET 1: PAIN POINTS

Scent longevity / throw concerns
Summary: Multiple customers mention the scent not lasting or not filling larger spaces. The underlying pain is paying for atmosphere and watching it evaporate — the product is beautiful to look at but its core function (scenting a room) has a ceiling.

Scent profile mismatch
Summary: At least one customer found the pine note overpowering. The pain is the gap between "forest-inspired" as a marketing idea and "too piney" as a lived experience in your home.

---

BUCKET 2: TRIGGER MOMENTS

Zoning a home by scent
Summary: Customers are buying the diffuser to differentiate rooms — bedroom vs. kitchen, quiet space vs. social space. The trigger is wanting each room to have its own atmospheric identity rather than one scent competing across the whole home.

Small-space living
Summary: Studio and small-apartment dwellers are reaching for the diffuser because it fits their square footage. The trigger is the match between product throw and actual room size.

---

BUCKET 3: OBJECTIONS BEFORE PURCHASING

Will it actually work in my space
Summary: Customers seem to be pre-evaluating whether the diffuser can perform in their specific square footage. This objection gets confirmed for small-space buyers and reinforced for larger-space buyers (the scent fades).

---

BUCKET 4: TRANSFORMATIONS

Bedroom as retreat
Summary: Customers are placing the diffuser in bedrooms specifically, suggesting it's being used to make the sleeping space feel like a distinct, restorative zone. The transformation is atmospheric — the bedroom becomes its own world.

Whole-home sensory escape (when paired)
Summary: When used alongside the Hearth candle across rooms, the diffuser contributes to a full-apartment transformation described as a getaway. The product shines in combination, not isolation.

Filling small spaces beautifully
Summary: For studio and small-apartment customers, the diffuser delivers on its atmosphere promise. The transformation is a small space that feels intentional and complete.
