## Pre-Session Setup

**Timing:** Before proposal/contract
**Duration:** 60-90 minutes
**Format:** Facilitated dialogue (recorded with consent)

### Preparation Checklist
- [ ] Review any materials client has shared (website, previous content, grant proposals)
- [ ] Note initial hypotheses about their assemblage
- [ ] Prepare recording equipment and backup
- [ ] Ground yourself—check sacral response to this client
- [ ] Set intention: witness, don't fix

### Environment Setup
- [ ] Quiet space with good acoustics
- [ ] Water available for client
- [ ] Timer set (but hidden from view)
- [ ] Recording consent form ready
- [ ] Note-taking system ready (minimal—stay present)

---

## Session Structure

### Opening (5 minutes)

**Set the container:**
> "This conversation is for me to understand your world—what you're building, what's working, what feels stuck. There are no wrong answers. I'm going to ask some questions that might feel unusual, but they help me understand how to best support you. Is it okay if I record this for my notes? You'll have full approval over anything that gets used publicly."

**Calibrate:**
- Notice their nervous system state
- Match energy initially, then gently regulate if needed
- Establish eye contact rhythm

---

### Domain 1: Current Narrative State (15-20 minutes)

**Opening Question:**
> "Walk me through how you currently explain your work to a stranger—like at a party, or to a potential funder who knows nothing about you."

**Listen for:**
- Where do they speed up or slow down?
- What do they skip over?
- Where do they hedge or qualify?
- What metaphors emerge naturally?

**Follow-up Probes:**
- "What's the gap between how you see yourselves and how you're seen?"
- "Where does your official story feel most true? Least true?"
- "If you could say anything about your work without consequences, what would you say?"
- "What do you wish people understood that they don't?"

### Domain 2: Organizational Assemblage (15-20 minutes)

**Transition:**
> "Now I want to understand the landscape you're operating in—the relationships, tensions, and dynamics that shape your work."

**Core Questions:**
- "What are the active tensions in your organization right now?"
- "Which stakeholder perspectives feel hardest to hold simultaneously?"
- "Who are you trying to speak to, and what do they need to hear?"
- "What's trying to emerge that your current structure can't accommodate?"

**Follow-up Probes:**
- "Tell me about a recent moment when communication broke down. What happened?"
- "Who in your ecosystem 'gets it' immediately? Who struggles?"
- "What would your most skeptical stakeholder say about this work?"


### Domain 3: Developmental Edges (15-20 minutes)

**Transition:**
> "I'm curious about the edges—the places where growth wants to happen but something's in the way."

**Core Questions:**
- "What would shift if you could express the full complexity of your work?"
- "What are you protecting by keeping certain stories private?"
- "Where does vulnerability feel risky versus generative?"
- "What's the conversation you've been avoiding?"

**Follow-up Probes:**
- "If this project is wildly successful, what becomes possible that isn't now?"
- "What's the fear underneath the fear?"
- "What truth are you not quite ready to speak?"

**Field Notes:**
```

```

---

### Domain 4: Readiness Assessment (10-15 minutes)

**Transition:**
> "Let me ask some practical questions about how you work and what would make this process successful."

**Core Questions:**
- "What would make this process feel extractive versus transformative?"
- "How do you currently handle organizational change?"
- "What's your relationship to being documented or seen?"
- "What does success look like for you in this work?"

**Follow-up Probes:**
- "Have you done anything like this before? What worked or didn't?"
- "Who else needs to be involved for this to land?"
- "What's your timeline pressure, if any?"
- "What's your budget reality?"

**Field Notes:**
```

Visibility comfort level:

Timeline:


Budget range:

```

---

### Closing (5-10 minutes)

**Reflect back:**
> "Here's what I'm hearing..." (2-3 sentence synthesis)

**Check:**
> "Does that land? What did I miss?"

**Next steps:**
> "I'll send you a summary of what we discussed and a proposal within [X days]. Before we end—any questions for me?"

**Final question:**
> "What's one thing you're taking away from this conversation?"

**Total Score:** ___ / 25

**Interpretation:**
- 20-25: High readiness, likely mid/high-tier engagement
- 15-19: Moderate readiness, may need Gateway first
- 10-14: Significant work needed, start with Facilitation Session (The Mirror)
