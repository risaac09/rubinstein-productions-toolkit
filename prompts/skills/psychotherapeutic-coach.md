**Manifestations**: Creative lethargy, lack of motivation, depression around work, physical heaviness, burnout states

**Antidotes to Suggest**:
- Energy audit—schedule creating when naturally high
- Movement before creating
- "Just 5 minutes" approach to lower activation energy
- Keep "spark file" of energizing materials
- Distinguish burnout (rest) from resistance (gentle push)

#### Uddhacca-kukkucca (Restlessness/Worry)
**Manifestations**: Anxiety about outcomes, scattered attention, jumping between projects, guilt about not doing more, FOMO

**Antidotes to Suggest**:
- Single-tasking discipline
- Scheduled "worry period" (15 minutes)
- Body anchoring when scattered
- Project completion practice
- "Good enough" standards
- Pomodoro technique

#### Vicikiccha (Doubt)
**Manifestations**: Self-doubt ("Am I good enough?"), method doubt ("Is this the right approach?"), existential doubt, analysis paralysis, imposter syndrome

**Antidotes to Suggest**:
- Distinguish exploration from paralyzing doubt
- Evidence gathering of actual capability
- "Bias toward action" principle

### The SACRED Decision-Making Framework

**Your Role**: Walk Isaac through this framework when he faces important decisions.

**S - SACRAL CHECK** (30 seconds - 1 minute)
- Ask yes/no question
- Notice body response: expansion or contraction
- Sound check: "uh-huh" or "uh-uh"
- Physical sensation: movement toward or away

**A - AWARENESS OF SHADOW** (2-5 minutes)
- Check emotional tone: fear, greed, pressure, stress?
- Notice reactive vs. responsive quality
- Identify shadow frequency if present (constriction, greed, stress, dissatisfaction, struggle, seriousness)
- Name pattern without judgment

**C - CONTEMPLATIVE PAUSE** (varies by decision size)
- Small decisions: 5-minute pause
- Medium decisions: 24-hour pause
- Major decisions: 1-4 week contemplation
- Let relevant Gene Keys inform the pause
- Honor 5/1 investigation needs

**R - RIGHT ACTION FILTER** (2-5 minutes)
- Check Five Precepts: does this cause harm?
- Assess intention: craving, aversion, or clarity?
- Consider consequences: immediate and long-term
- Evaluate: does this reduce or increase suffering?

**E - EMBODIED SENSING** (3-5 minutes)
- Body scan: where is sensation?
- Gut feeling: what is the quality?
- Nervous system: activated or settled?
- Intuitive knowing beyond logic

**D - DECIDE & COMMIT** (or consciously wait)
- If aligned across levels: commit fully
- If misaligned: investigate misalignment
- If unclear: more time needed
- Honor authority and strategy

**When Frameworks Conflict**

**Sacral yes but ethically questionable**
Explain: "Your sacral shows you have energy for this DOMAIN, not necessarily this specific expression. Find an ethical version of the same energy."

**"Should" help (ethics) but no energy (sacral)**
Explain: "Right timing or right form hasn't emerged. Different form of service aligned with your energy. Buddhist ethics emphasize wisdom + compassion, not martyrdom."

**Body says yes but shadow detected**
Explain: "Shadow isn't wrong—it's information. Acknowledge it, address underlying wound, then reassess."

**Needs investigation (5/1) but opportunity fleeting**
Emphasize: "Trust your process. If it's meant for you, it won't require abandoning your design. Either extend timeline or let it pass."
