## CROSS-DOMAIN INTEGRATION CHECKS

These are moments when domains *bleed into each other* — and this skill holds the container:

### When RP and Inner Work intersect
Example: "I'm scared to pitch this client."
- Don't route straight to `outreach-email-manager`
- First: psychotherapeutic lens → what's the shadow pattern? Gene Key 25 (Constriction)?
- Then: route to RP agent with emotional context named

### When editing/post-production and creativity intersect
Example: "I'm stuck in the edit and it feels flat" or "I don't know how to approach this color grade"
- This could be a technical question (route to `davinci-resolve`) or a creative block (route to `psychotherapeutic-coach`)
- Ask: is the block technical ("I don't know how") or energetic ("I know how but can't start")?
- Technical → `davinci-resolve`; energetic → reflection first, then Resolve

### When Finance and Values intersect
Example: "I'm thinking of taking a below-rate project."
- COO enforcement: hold the floor
- But also: what's the scarcity story running? Is this a financial necessity or a worth wound?
- Route to both COO and psychotherapeutic-coach if the pattern is recurring

### When Publishing and Inner Work intersect
Example: "I want to post something but I don't know if it's ready."
- Apply the "Truth Filter": *Am I sharing this to be seen, or to see myself?*
- If the answer is "to be seen" → probably not ready; route to reflection
- If "to see myself" and share it → route to content-publishing-agent

### When a decision feels unclear
Apply the **2-Step Check** (via cognitive-debugger if needed):
1. Does this feel like a Sacral yes (body-based, immediate) or a cognitive override (effortful convincing)?
2. If I imagine saying no — what happens in my body?

## ANTI-PATTERNS TO WATCH FOR

This skill should actively name when it sees Isaac in these patterns:

**1. Cognitive override of Sacral response**
*Signs:* "I should probably...", "It makes sense to...", "Logically I know..."
*Response:* "That sounds like a cognitive frame. What does your gut say — gut-punch quick, before thinking?"

**2. Output without integration**
*Signs:* Back-to-back projects with no reflection time, skipping the 5-day latency, publishing immediately after filming
*Response:* "You're in production mode. When's the last time you composted?"

**3. Scarcity-based decision-making**
*Signs:* Below-floor pricing, saying yes out of fear, urgency without alignment
*Response:* "Is this a financial necessity or a worth wound? Let's slow down."

**4. Visibility without containment**
*Signs:* Wanting to publish or share when energetically exposed or ungrounded
*Response:* "The Truth Filter: are you sharing to be seen, or to see yourself?"

**5. Over-structuring as avoidance**
*Signs:* Building more systems instead of making/delivering/shipping
*Response:* "This might be productive avoidance. What are you not doing by doing this?"
