# Newsletter — Monthly Dispatch

The newsletter is RP's owned-audience channel. Not a content marketing engine. Not a funnel. A monthly written dispatch to people who have asked to hear from Isaac directly.

If IG or YouTube cool tomorrow, the newsletter is the relationship that persists.

---

## Shape

**Monthly.** First week of each month. One issue.

**Length:** 400–800 words. Dense, not skimmable. If it can be skimmed, it's too long.

**Structure** (not a template — a skeleton):
1. One thing that shifted this month (in the practice, in thinking, in the field).
2. One piece of work — an engagement, a film, an experiment — described honestly.
3. One open question.

No rotating sections. No "what I'm reading." No roundups. The newsletter is metabolized thinking, not aggregation.

---

## Voice

Read like a letter to a specific person, not a broadcast. Same voice as RP writing generally: declarative, somatic, specific, slightly slower than most newsletters. No urgency manufactured. No CTAs at the bottom.

If a sale or a new offer is relevant, it goes in a P.S., never in the body.

---

## Audience & growth

**Who subscribes:** past clients, collaborators, people who've watched the work for a while and want a quieter channel.

**How it grows:** a single line at the bottom of rubinsteinproductions.com, one mention per quarter on IG, and direct invitations. No lead magnets, no pop-ups, no growth tactics.

**Size target:** none. Unsubscribes are healthy — they signal the dispatch is specific enough that not everyone wants it.

---

## Platform

Start with a simple service (Buttondown, Beehiiv, or plain Mailchimp). Do not start on Substack — the network discovery features pull toward content marketing logic.

Own the list export from day one. Never lock in.

---

## Operating rhythm

| When | What |
|---|---|
| Last week of month | Draft the upcoming issue (60–90 min) |
| First Monday of month | Send |
| Month-end | Note open rate + any replies in `stack-data/data/content.json` channel entry |

**If a month passes and there's nothing to send:** send a 100-word note naming that directly. One skip per year is acceptable. Two in a row is a signal to reassess.

---

## What this is NOT

- A content-repurposing vehicle (IG → newsletter reposting)
- A sales channel
- A place to announce things that should have been announced directly to specific people
- A place to perform aliveness when the practice is dormant

---
