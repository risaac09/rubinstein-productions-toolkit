# Financial Operations

## Revenue Tracking

Track all revenue by tier and quarter using the `invoice-financial-tracker` skill or manually in a spreadsheet.

### Monthly Cadence

| Task | When | Tool |
|------|------|------|
| Invoice active clients | 1st of month (retainers) or upon delivery (projects) | Invoice template |
| Follow up on outstanding invoices | 15th of month | Email |
| Log all business expenses | Weekly | Expense tracker |
| Set aside tax reserve | Upon receipt of each payment | 30% to savings |
| Review monthly P&L | Last day of month | Spreadsheet |

### Invoicing Standards

- Invoice within 48 hours of deliverable completion
- Payment terms: Net-30 (default) or 50/50 split (50% upon signing, 50% upon delivery)
- Territory retainers: invoice on the 1st, due by the 15th
- Always include: project name, deliverables completed, payment terms, bank details or payment link
- Follow up once at 15 days overdue, once at 30 days, then escalate

### Tax Reserve

Set aside 30% of every payment received into a separate account. This covers:

- Federal self-employment tax (~15.3%)
- Federal income tax (estimated marginal rate)
- State taxes (Washington: no income tax, but B&O tax applies)

**Quarterly estimated tax payments:** Due April 15, June 15, September 15, January 15. Use the reserve to pay these.

## Expense Categories

| Category | Examples | Tax Deductible |
|----------|---------|----------------|
| **Equipment** | Camera, audio gear, lighting, computer | Yes (depreciate or Section 179) |
| **Software** | DaVinci Resolve, Adobe, Zoom, AI tools | Yes |
| **Shipping** | Camera kit shipping, return labels | Yes |
| **Travel** | Client site visits, conferences | Yes (keep receipts) |
| **Professional development** | Courses, workshops, books | Yes |
| **Insurance** | Business liability, equipment insurance | Yes |
| **Home office** | Percentage of rent/mortgage, internet | Yes (calculated annually) |
| **Subscriptions** | GitHub, hosting, domain | Yes |
| **Meals** | Client meals (50% deductible) | Partial |

### Expense Logging Rules

- Log every expense within 7 days of purchase
- Keep digital receipts (photo or PDF) for everything over $25
- Categorize at time of logging — don't batch categorization

## Quarterly Review Financial Checklist

Run this at the start of each quarter:

1. Actual revenue vs. target (by tier)
2. Tax reserve status — is the 30% set aside current?
3. Outstanding invoices — anyone past 30 days?
4. Expense review — any categories creeping up?
5. Cash flow projection for next quarter
6. Pricing review — any below-floor deals this quarter? Pattern in pushback?
