The Client Journey

Seven stages from recognition to return — the full arc of voice liberation work.

Stage Details

01 Recognition
"They find us."
The client encounters RP through content, referral, or the toolkit. Something resonates — they recognize their own narrative dysregulation in the language. This is not marketing; it's pattern recognition.
ChannelsToolkit, LinkedIn, referral, speaking
DeliverableNone — they come to us
Pre-engagement
02 Conversation
"Mutual fit."
An informal discovery call — not a sales pitch. Both sides assess alignment. We listen for readiness: are they buying a service, or are they willing to be changed? The sacral responds or it doesn't.
Duration30–45 min call
DeliverableClarity on fit; Mirror Session Prep Guide if proceeding
Pre-engagement
03 Mirror
"Listen and reflect."
The first deep engagement. A 90-minute facilitated session where we hold the mirror — the client hears their own voice reflected back through narrative synthesis. Diagnostic by nature, not prescriptive.
Duration90-minute session + synthesis
DeliverableMirror Narrative document
Container
04 Map
"Co-design the engagement."
Together we scope the work: timeline, pricing, Bilingual Dashboard metrics. The Map stage bridges diagnosis and action — building the architecture for the engagement based on what the Mirror revealed.
Duration1–2 weeks scoping
DeliverablesEngagement Proposal, Project Roadmap, Bilingual Dashboard framework
Container Excavation
05 Integration
"The core work."
Where transformation happens. Facilitation, filming, data collection — all threaded through the Bilingual Dashboard. The client moves from awareness to embodiment. We hold both the institutional frame and the relational field.
DurationMulti-session engagement
DeliverablesSession facilitation, footage, Progress Check-ins, Dashboard updates
Excavation Performance
06 Story Travels
"Post-production."
The raw material becomes artifact. Editing, reports, final Bilingual Dashboard — the voice that was excavated now travels in forms the client can use. The 5-day latency ensures integration, not just output.
Duration2–4 weeks post-production
DeliverablesFinal Delivery Package, Bilingual Dashboard Report, edited media
Translation
07 Return
"The door stays open."
Offboarding with gratitude. A Thank-You letter, an open invitation to return. Many clients cycle back — the journey is a spiral, not a line. Referrals often begin here.
DeliverableThank-You & Offboarding letter
OngoingAnnual check-in, referral relationship
Post-engagement
