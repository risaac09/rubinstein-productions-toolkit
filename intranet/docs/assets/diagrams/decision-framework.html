Decision Framework

How we triage opportunities — from first glance to final commitment.

Layer 1 — A/B/C Triage

A
Immediate
Active lead, clear fit, ready to scope. The sacral says yes on first contact. Move fast — respond within 24 hours.
→ Proceed to 6-Question Filter
B
Nurture
Interesting but not ready. Could be timing, budget, or readiness. Add to pipeline and follow up in 2 weeks. Don't chase — stay warm.
→ Pipeline + 2-week follow-up
C
Decline
Poor fit, below floor rate, or capacity exceeded. Decline gracefully with a referral if possible. No guilt — protecting capacity protects quality.
→ Graceful decline + referral

Layer 2 — The Six Gates

For A-category opportunities only. All six must pass.

1
Does the sacral respond?
Gut yes or no. If you're thinking yourself into a yes, it's a no. The Generator strategy is non-negotiable — the body knows before the mind does.
2
Does it meet the floor rate?
$150/hr minimum, non-negotiable. Below this, the work cannot be sustained at the quality it requires. No exceptions.
3
Is there capacity?
Max 2 Territory clients, 4 Map engagements per quarter, 3 active projects simultaneously. Check the dashboard before committing.
4
Is the client ready for the work?
Not just buying a service — willing to be changed. Look for curiosity over consumption. If they want deliverables without process, it's a mismatch.
5
Can we deliver in 5-day latency rhythm?
Creative deliverables need integration time. If the timeline doesn't allow for the 5-business-day latency between delivery and next action, renegotiate scope.
6
Does it advance the phase roadmap?
Foundation → Leverage → Scale. Does this project build toward the current phase? Interesting work that doesn't advance the roadmap dilutes momentum.
All 6 YES → Accept the engagement
Any NO → Negotiate scope or decline gracefully
Generator Energy Principle
If weekly energy is below 6/10, no new commitments regardless of opportunity quality. Energy is the ultimate constraint — everything else is downstream of it. At 6–7: only A-category. At 8+: full capacity available.
