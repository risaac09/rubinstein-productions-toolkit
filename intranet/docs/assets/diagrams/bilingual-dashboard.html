Reading Both Registers Together

Both High (Convergence)
The engagement is working on all levels. Satisfaction scores are high AND relational dynamics are shifting. This is the target state.
Royal High / Nomadic Low
Looks successful on paper but nothing fundamental changed. The facilitation may have been too comfortable — not enough excavation.
Royal Low / Nomadic High
Something profound is happening but it's not showing up in metrics yet. Nomadic indicators are leading indicators — Royal will catch up.
