## What this practice is

Rubinstein Productions — a facilitation and film practice for mission-driven people and organizations whose capacity for honest expression has been alienated by the systems they operate inside. The condition is structural, not personal. The work is relational, not heroic. The offering is physical and anti-extractive: ship a camera, the client films themselves, everything is returned.

Public site: [rubinsteinproductions.com](https://rubinsteinproductions.com)

## What this practice is NOT

- Not coaching, messaging strategy, or branding
- Not corporate mindfulness
- Not video production (the camera is a witness)
- Not a framework you install

## The methodology (four phases)

**Container → Excavation → Performance → Translation**

1. **Container** — Nervous-system safety + institutional permission (PIPA logic model)
2. **Excavation** — Surface the inside version (CBSD feedback-loop mapping)
3. **Performance** — Camera ships to client; they film themselves on their terms
4. **Translation** — 3-min brand video + raw footage returned + Bilingual Dashboard

Deep-dive: [Methodology — Four Phases](../learning/four-phases.md). Note: the methodology pages still reference older stage names in places — see drift note in [Architecture](../architecture.md).

## The Bilingual Dashboard

Outcomes measured in two registers simultaneously: institutional metrics (completion, ROI, satisfaction, narrative clarity) and relational indicators (reciprocity shifts, narrative consistency, somatic safety, line-of-flight emergence). Most consultants speak one register. RP speaks both.

## Voice rules

The Say Why brand uses:

- "Facilitator and filmmaker" (primary identity)
- "Information Alchemist" (meta-identity)
- "The story travels without me"
- "Honest expression, not performance"
- Embodied/somatic vocabulary, Deleuzian internal references where load-bearing

The Say Why brand does **not** use: surveillance-evaluation language, MPH-credentialing register, generic "consultant" framing, promotional adjectives, or anything that crosses into the [Independent Evaluator](evaluation.md) practice's voice.

## Cross-practice firewall

This practice and [Independent Evaluation](evaluation.md) share an operator and back-of-house operations (financial, capacity, IT, BD pipeline) but enforce hard separation in client-facing voice, website, and methodology naming. A Say Why client should not see institutional evaluation language. Drift between the two is the highest reputation risk to either practice.
