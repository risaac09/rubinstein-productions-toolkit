# Welcome to Rubinstein Productions

If you're reading this, you're joining a practice that sits at the intersection of facilitation, film, and honest expression. This onboarding section will orient you to how RP works, what the tools and systems are, and how to get up to speed.

---

## What RP Does

Rubinstein Productions helps mission-driven organizations say what they've been afraid to say. We do this through a combination of facilitated conversation, participant-led filmmaking, and bilingual measurement — tracking outcomes in both institutional and relational languages.

The methodology moves through four phases: **Container** (create safety) → **Excavation** (listen deeply) → **Performance** (capture truth) → **Translation** (edit, return, let the story travel).

The competitive edge is the **Bilingual Dashboard** — our ability to deliver reports that speak both the language funders need (Royal Metrics) and the language that captures what actually happened (Nomadic Indicators).

## Who Isaac Is

Isaac Rubinstein is the founder and sole practitioner (for now). His background includes an MPH in healthcare systems, experience in facilitation and program delivery, and deep investment in the intersection of philosophy, creative practice, and organizational change.
